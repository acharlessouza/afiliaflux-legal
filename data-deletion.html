AfiliaFlux Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2026-06-27

AfiliaFlux is a private/internal application used to monitor and process interactions on the Instagram professional account memandaoachado. It is not offered as a public SaaS product.

How to request data deletion

To request deletion of data associated with this app, send an email to anthony18meister@gmail.com with the subject "AfiliaFlux Data Deletion Request".

Information to include

Include enough information to identify the related Instagram interaction, such as Instagram username, comment date, publication/Reel link, or a description of the interaction.

What will be deleted

After receiving a valid request, the app operator will remove applicable stored webhook events, public comment data, public identifiers, timestamps, and related internal records associated with the request.

Contact

For questions about data deletion, contact anthony18meister@gmail.com.
