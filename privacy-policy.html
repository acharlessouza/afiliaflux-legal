AfiliaFlux Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-27

AfiliaFlux is a private/internal application used to monitor and process interactions on the Instagram professional account memandoachado. It is not offered as a public SaaS product.

Data collected

The application may collect public comments made on monitored Instagram posts/Reels, public identifiers required to process those comments, timestamps, webhook metadata, and technical payloads received from Meta.

How data is used

Data is used only to detect relevant comments on monitored publications, register received events for operational auditing/debugging, and trigger configured automatic replies or private replies.

Data sharing

AfiliaFlux does not sell collected data and does not share it with third parties except when required to operate the authorized Meta/Instagram API integration or comply with legal obligations.

Data retention and deletion

Data is retained only for internal operation, auditing, and debugging of the application. To request deletion of related data, contact the application responsible at anthony18meister@gmail.com.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact anthony18meister@gmail.com.
